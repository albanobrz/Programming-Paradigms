There is 3 players in the game.
Each player gets 2 cards each round.
Each round, all the players cards are compared, the highest rank wins.
If the winning rank has more than 1 event, the game is decided by the card suit.
The suit winning order is this Spades > Hearts > Diamonds > Clubs.
When the deck runs out of cards, the game is over.
At the end of the game, who has more rounds won, is the big winner.

Cards power:
A > K > Q > J > 10 > 9 > 8 > 7 > 6 > 5 > 4 > 3 > 2
